Appeal

Gundalpet is located in the Chamrajnagar District of Karnataka. More than 3,000 Muslims live here. The Darul Salaam Educational Charitable Trust was established in 2006 as a major initiative for the multifaceted development of this educationally and economically backward region. Two mosques, an Arabic & Urdu madrassa with more than 300 students, Excellent public school, a women's Qur'an center and a ladies' tailoring institute, and a boarding school with around 30 students, adopting and providing hostel facilities with their full expenses. Our trust distributes textbooks to needy children and sewing machines to girls, and During Eidul Adha it distributes Uduhiyyat meat to thousands of families and during Ramadan fasting food and food kits. The aim of the trust is to uplift a country that is socially and economically backward. Maintenance of mosques Salaries and meals currently cost around Rs. 3 lakhs a month. Our only reliance is on the generous helping hand of this institution which has no regular income. Every day the children pray for helpers. We kindly invite you and your family to see this organization, while requesting that some of your sweat drops help in the upliftment of this institution
